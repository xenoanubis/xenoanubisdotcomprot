Sub heading

On its most basic, metaphysical level the universe Tynara inhabits is made up of interwoven strands of elemental energy, referred to as the Aetheric weave, the song of the Eternals made manifest.

Or at least, that's the best understanding the beings existing there can come to.

In truth no being within the song truly understands the nature of reality's foundational dimension. Higher dimensional beings KNOW it's there, feel it as the boundary that defines everything, and can gather some understanding of how it works, but none know its full scope, nor can even the gods touch or manipulate its behavior. The weave has three main truths.\

  • 1. It exists.
  • 2. It defines all parts of reality in some way, from the physical shape of things to behavior of energies, to the fate of nations.
  • 3. It “moves” differently depending on which of the three greater dimensional realms it is in. It is nearly static in the heavenly realm, a rhythmic flexing in the midverse, and a flailing mess in the Abyss.

Subheading

The Etheric plane, or the Etheric Layers as it is sometimes more accurately called, or the Etheric Functionary Planes if you want a mouthful, is the sum total of insubstantial reality that make-up Tynara's universe.

If that sounds like as much of an obtuse description as the Aetheric Layer of reality, that's because it is. At least to a normal physical mortal mind.

What makes the Etheric Layers different is primarily that beings can interact and sometimes perceive parts of it, unlike the Weave. Interaction with the Etheric planes comes it two modes: Actively shifting one's entire being into the Etheric by converting it into the magical energy known as maga; an act of powerful magic typically only usable by beings of the First Creation, or projecting one's mind across dimensions, a feat more easily managed by even mortal mages and even some without arcane training. Magics tied to perception in the Etheric are technically lesser forms of the second mode. Progression between layers typically requires more effort as one goes further outward. The Etheric layers are best described as radiating outward from a point centered on the substantial planes. Most beings describe a distinction of inner, outer, and middle layers. The exact bounds of each layer, however, even with common Etheric regions, is difficult. These dimensional layers are not, technically, “static”. They move like innumerable silk veils, hung in thin rows.

Inner Etheric planes are closest to the substantial realms and are easiest to perceive or even shift into by beings, known as becoming ethereal. Most active magic energies exist in inner etheric layers. When observed from a state within them, these planes typically are strange reflections of the area local to where the entity entered, often missing aspects such as color, sound, or visual depth, becoming more distorted the further outward in the etheric one goes. This is because, while the various layers are still universal, Inner layers are localized to the substance they bound, meaning 2 entities in rough analogous parts of 2 separate Substantial planes viewing the Inner Etheric will never interact or perceive each other.

The Middle Etheric, by contrast, can truly bound multiple physical points across various Substantial Planes at once, thus the Middle Etheric is often considered the first crossroads of reality. Visual perception of the Middle Etheric planes often manifests as a boundless expanse of vague gray mist where occasional impressions of objects or places may form in the vapor.

Very little is known about the Outer Etheric, except that beings that can travel the Etheric cannot cross into these regions, and projecting one's perception into them is fairly difficult. The reason for this is while the soul of living things has some presence in closer layers, the Outer Etheric is where the soul truly exists within the mechanics of the universe. The only entities capable of operating within the Outer Etheric are angels, who have no soul and are pure magic.

To the senses, the Outer Etheric is strange and abstract. The layers here are reactive to thought, emotion and memory, and since the souls of every being that exists and every mortal or balanced soul that ever has existed is rooted here, billions of points of influence are constantly reflected across the landscape, often overlapping and merging to the untrained observer.
