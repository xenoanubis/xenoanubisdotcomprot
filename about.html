About

    The entity known as Xenoanubis, or Xeno, or any number of names and titles it has come to be called (for those of absolute necessity; he/him/it), is a being of archaic origin and nebulous intent. The Truth

    Cast adrift in the winding air ducts of space and time, it occasionally pokes it's furred tendrils through the grates of the modern world, more often than not to listen, but increasingly to babble its ravings into the minds of passers by. At no point should anything it says be taken seriously or to heart, as its thoughts are often ill defined, missing in context, and badly articulated.
